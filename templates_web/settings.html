How to get a Gmail App Password
  1. Enable 2-Step Verification on your Google account at myaccount.google.com/security.
  2. Go to myaccount.google.com/apppasswords.
  3. Click Select app → choose "Mail", then Select device → "Other (custom name)".
  4. Name it Nexus Mail and click Generate.
  5. Copy the 16-character password and paste it in the field above.
